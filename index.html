About

Know more about us



The Logeeks 'A'-Tutors (Scientific) is an establishment, which offers Tutorials on Science Subjects, to Senior Secondary Schools Students and Candidates of SSCE (WAEC, NECO), JAMB and A'level Examinations
We have our specialization in Science -for better efficiency- to transform Students into mind-blowing Scientists. We tutor not just to pass Exams, but beyond that, making 'Science' lively and lovely to make our Students #GET DEVELOPED

FAQs

How can I get a home Tutor?

Click on 'Tutorials' in the Menu bar, and then see the 'PRIVATE TUTORIALS' section to get started.

How can I become a Tutor?

To become one of the Logeeks 'A'-Tutors, send your CV to our email address along side your Application letter (Don't forget to include your salient skills and experiences in your CV). We will send you a reply as soon as possible. Thanks.
